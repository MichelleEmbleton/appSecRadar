<p class='legend-text'>

<span class='key-heading'>Technology States in the Radars</span><br /><br />
The radar serves as a one-stop-shop for making appsec security decisions about technologies to use in your applications.
<br /><br />
<span class='key-red'>HOLD</span><br />
Stable AppSec Technology that is currently not planned to be phased out, and recommended for continued use.
<br /><br />
<span class='key-orange'>ADOPT</span><br />
AppSec Technology that is new but should be adopted to account for a changing threat landscape.
<br /><br />
<span class='key-cadmium-yellow'>TRIAL</span><br />
AppSec technology that should be trialled and AppSec teams are recommended to provide feedback on the new technology.
<br /><br />
<span class='key-lemon-yellow'>EVALUATE</span><br />
Technology that is too early for production use, however AppSec teams are encouraged to PoC.
<br /><br />
<span class='key-yellow-green'>NO NEW USE</span><br />
Tech teams should not use this technology for new projects.
<br /><br />
<span class='key-lime-green'>PLAN FOR REMOVAL</span><br />
Technology that is nearing End of Life (EoL) or is soon to be deprecated. AppSec teams should be actively planning for removal of such technology.
<br /><br />
